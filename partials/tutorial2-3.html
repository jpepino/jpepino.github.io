Mary Had a Little Lamb

With only three notes, it is now possible to learn our song. Use what you learned in the previous section to follow the notes above, to the melody of the well-known "Mary Had a Little Lamb."

Let's also take the opportunity to learn more about note duration. Recall that the time signature states that there are four beats in a measure. The black notes with stems that we have been using so far are called quarter notes, and in a 4/4 time signature are worth one beat. The white notes with stems are with double that of the quarter notes, or two beats, and are called half notes. The white note without a stem (resembling a donut), is worth 4 beats and is called a whole note.
