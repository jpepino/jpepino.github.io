First Three Notes

The first three notes we will look at memorizing are E, F, and G. The lowest note above is E, the middle note is an F, and the highest note is a G. As we approach the higher lines in the musical staff, the notes progress alphabetically. Conversely, as we approach the lower lines the notes regress alphabetically back towards A.
