First Five Notes

A staff, in music, is a set of five horizontal lines and the four open spaces in between the lines. Each of these lines and open spaces represents a different musical pitch. In music, the pitch means how high or low the sound is. Pitches can be represented in notation by musical notes, which serve as the building blocks of written music.

Notes define two things in music: the pitch, which was mentioned before, and the duration. A musical note can be held out for varying lengths of time, which can be accounted for in musical notation. How to read duration will be covered in a later lesson.
