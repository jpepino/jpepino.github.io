Completing the C Scale

Let's add the final three notes to complete our C scale. A scale is a simply defined as a set of notes, typically listed from low to high pitch. There are different scales which define what notes are played in a certain key signature.

The C scale, as picture in the graphic above, begins and ends on C. We know the first five notes already from previous lessons, but the final three notes (in order) are A, B, and C.
